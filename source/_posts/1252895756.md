3初始化文件系统
========

3.1初始化为ext类型
------------

    mkfs.ext4 /dev/mapper/centos-home
    

![](https://img2023.cnblogs.com/blog/1446132/202310/1446132-20231012165136867-451127871.png)

3.2初始化为xfs类型
------------

    mkfs.xfs /dev/mapper/centos-home
    

因为我已经把文件类型转换为了ext，后期转换成xfs需要加上-f

![](https://img2023.cnblogs.com/blog/1446132/202310/1446132-20231012171906720-726653875.png)

4挂载home
=======

4.1临时挂载home
-----------

    mount /dev/mapper/centos-home /home
    

![](https://img2023.cnblogs.com/blog/1446132/202310/1446132-20231012165312246-2044197418.png)

4.2永久挂载home
-----------

设置永久时一点要注意不要随意添加，会导致系统无法启动，注意系统备份

    vi /etc/fstab
    

![](https://img2023.cnblogs.com/blog/1446132/202310/1446132-20231012172223347-958575214.png)
